About me

Hey there, let me tell you a few things about myself

I'm Samuel Orobosa Amagbakhen, a creative front-end developer who is passionate about digital technology and its future in our present day world. I love creating things that live and breathe on the web with the tools available to me. I am a fast learner and I love to get involved with projects so I can learn.

I am currently a student of The University of Benin where I major in Computer Science. I am also an active volunteer for Junior Chamber International,Nigeria(UNIBEN chapter) and a member of Facebook Developers Circle, Benin. I also hold a certification in Responsive Web Design from freeCodeCamp.

I also have great stress and time management as I deliver before deadlines set for me without giving in to pressure.

Although I love coding, I also fancy educating and teaching others what I know in any way I can. When I'm not coding or changing the world, I fancy soccer, basketball and soul music. Find me interesting? Then contact me. You'll be glad you worked with me.
